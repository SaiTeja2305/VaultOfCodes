🍫 Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

👩‍🍳 Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir well.
  4. Pour mixture into baking pan.
  5. Bake for 25–30 minutes.
  6. Let it cool and serve!
